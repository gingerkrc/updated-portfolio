About Me

Greetings portfolio viewers! Let me introduce myself and give you a bit of information about me. My name is Ginger Richards Conly. I was born and raised in Boulder, CO and now live in Denver, CO with my husband, two children, and two cats. I attended the University of Colorado at Denver, where I received a Bachelor of Science in Music with a minor in studio arts. After several years of working in public libraries, I moved to Washington DC and received a Master of Science in Library Science with a specialization in Music Librarianship at Catholic Univeristy of America. Beyond my academic and professional pursuits, I have performed music since I was a child and have played in several garage, punk, and indie-pop bands in both Denver and Washington DC. Currently I am building my portfolio as a web developer, which has been allowing me to exercise my analytical skills along with my creativity.
