About Me

I grew up in Boulder, CO and have been fixated with music from a young age. In junior high I received a large collection of discarded music magazines from a librarian who was aware of my obsession, thus seeding my interest in library science. Upon receiving a Bachelor of Science in Music from the University of Colorado at Denver and working in public libraries, I eventually moved to Washington, DC where I received a Master of Science in Library Science with a specialization in Music Librarianship.
